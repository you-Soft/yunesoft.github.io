The Garment District Inc.

In Brief

A garment manufacturing group established in December 2002, is in the business of supply and manufacture items for both local and export market.

The company would like to promote professionalism in the practice of garment manufacturing evidenced by product and service quality, workmanship rendered by trained, skillful work force, and application of systems and procedures in the operations.

The Garment District is housed in a 1,200 m2 warehouse facility in the factory Sunny Realty Compound, Sandoval Avenue, Pasig City.


Manufacture and supply of garments

The company supplies various garment items for men's, ladies and children's such as basic and high fashion denim pants, cargo pants, tokong and shorts, capri pants and bermuda shorts for ladies and girls, basic and high fashion jackets, shirts, blouses and children's dresses . Specialty items that are garment-related like bags and belts can also be supplied based on customer's requirements.


Uniforms and company-specific products

The company extends its services in the manufacture and supply of uniforms & promotional products.







Performance and Quality

A member of the Philippine Total Quality Management (TQM), benchmarking group for small and medium scale establishments, the company continues to operate in alignment with SA 8000 and possibly ISO 8000 in the near future.

The Process

Product Development

In garment manufacturing the first step is designing the sketch. Working drawings help the pattern makers in understanding the patterns involved in the construction. Moreover, our team develops the first pattern for the designs in any one standard size. The pattern is then graded for size sets and marked for optimum fabric layout.

Cut & Construction

The fabric is then cut with the help of cloth cutting machines suitable for the type of the cloth. The sorter sorts the patterns according to size and design and makes bundles of them. The sorted bundles of fabrics are now ready to be stitched. The garments are pieced together in an assembly-line procedure for maximum efficiency.

Rinse / Wash

Finished pieces are sent out for washing. Garments need to be washed to avoid shrinkage as well as remove adhering dirt, dust, and other unwanted debris. Different types of washes may be applied, such as enzyme, acid, or cloud wash, to obtain unique effects. Washed garments also produce a softer feel to the touch and more comfortable fit.

Finishing & Accessories

Accessories and labels are finally attached to the garments for a more finished look. Customers may choose among various colors and designs of buttons, zippers, trinkets, and labels to suit their style. Accessories may be customized to bear customer's brand names, and company logos. Every piece is unique to you.

Quality Check

The production group operates under the guidance and supervision of an indepentend Quality Control group who sets the high quality standards by which the production group of the company operates on. This sets the workmanship at par with the quality of imported goods.

Delivery

The finished garments are finally sorted on the basis of design and size and packed to send for distribution to the retail outlets. Logo printing or embroidery, packaging, and even delivery shall be provided to complete the service.
