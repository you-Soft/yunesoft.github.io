Mr. Nabil Sleiman, Ph.D, CFA

Chief Investment Officer

Mr. Nabil Sleiman, Ph.D., CFA, is Chief Investment Officer/Co-Founder of Aldhabi Investment PJSC since 2010.

Mr. Nabil has over twenty years of experience in the hedge fund industry. Before joining Aldhabi Investment PJSC, Nabil was Chief Investment Officer of SFG Asset Advisors, a family office Global Macro hedge fund based in San Francisco CA from 2005-2009.

Before that he was co-portfolio manager at Manning & Napier Advisors (2002-2004) co-managing the M&N top-down International Fund.

Prior to that, Mr. Nabil worked at Abu Dhabi Investment Authority (ADIA) from 1995 to 2001 as deputy portfolio manager of the External Funds Department.

Mr. Nabil holds a Ph.D. in International Studies from the Josef Korbel School of International Studies, University of Denver, along with an MBA in Finance, and a Master’s degree in International Management from the University of Denver, Daniels College of Business.

He is a CFA charter holder and a member of the Security Analysts of San Francisco.
